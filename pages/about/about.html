About EasyGo

Transforming transportation across South Africa

Our Story

EasyGo was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make transportation in South Africa safe, reliable, and affordable for everyone. What started as a small startup in Johannesburg has grown into a platform serving thousands of riders and drivers across the country.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, safe, and efficient transportation solutions that connect people and communities across South Africa.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and innovative mobility platform, empowering drivers and delighting riders.

Our Values

Safety first, customer focus, driver respect, innovation, and community impact guide everything we do.
