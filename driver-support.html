Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by going to Settings > Profile Information. From there, you can edit your name, contact details, address, and other personal information. Don't forget to click Save after making changes.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to Settings > Privacy & Security. Enter your current password, then your new password twice, and click "Change Password". Make sure your new password is at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters and numbers.

When will I get paid?

Payouts are processed weekly every Monday for all rides completed in the previous week (Monday to Sunday). You can also opt for daily payouts in Settings > Payment Settings. Payments are sent to your selected payout method within 24-48 hours.

How do I update my payout method?

Go to Settings > Payment Settings to update your payout method. You can add bank accounts, cards, or PayPal. Make sure all details are correct to avoid payment delays. You can also set a primary payout method and backup methods.

How do I cancel a ride?

To cancel a ride, go to your Current Trip and click the Cancel button. Please note that frequent cancellations may affect your driver rating. If you need to cancel due to an emergency, please contact support immediately.

Can I set preferred areas?

Yes! Go to Settings > Driver Preferences to set your preferred areas. You can select multiple areas where you prefer to drive. The app will prioritize showing you rides in these areas when you're online.

How do I report vehicle issues?

If you're experiencing vehicle issues, go to Support > Submit Ticket and select "Vehicle Issue" as the category. Describe the problem and upload photos if applicable. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours.

How do I schedule maintenance?

You can schedule maintenance reminders in the Vehicle section. Go to My Vehicles, select your vehicle, and click "Schedule Maintenance". Set the type of maintenance, due date, and get reminders when it's time.

What safety features are available?

EasyGo provides several safety features including: emergency button during rides, ride tracking, driver verification, and 24/7 support. You can also share your trip status with emergency contacts. In case of emergency, use the SOS button in the app.

How do I report a safety concern?

If you have a safety concern, immediately use the emergency button in the app. For non-emergency safety issues, submit a ticket with the "Safety Concern" category or call our dedicated safety hotline at +27 80 123 4567.
